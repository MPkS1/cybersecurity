Beginner Level

  • Introduction to Cybersecurity: Common threats, attack vectors, and security mindset
  • Networking Fundamentals: IP addressing, firewalls, VPNs, and secure protocols
  • Operating Systems Security: Securing Windows, Linux, and Mac systems

Intermediate Level

  • Cryptography: Encryption methods, hashing algorithms, and PKI
  • Web Application Security: SQL injection, XSS, CSRF, and prevention methods

Advanced Level

  • Penetration Testing & Ethical Hacking: Using tools like Metasploit, Nmap, and Wireshark
  • Digital Forensics: Evidence collection, analysis techniques, and reporting

Expert Level

  • Compliance and Best Practices: ISO 27001, NIST frameworks, and GDPR
  • Projects & Practical Experience: CTF challenges, bug bounties, and real-world applications

Learning Resources

As you progress through each level, consider these resources to deepen your knowledge:

  • Online courses from platforms like Coursera, Udemy, and edX
  • Hands-on labs through TryHackMe and HackTheBox
  • Cybersecurity certifications (CompTIA Security+, CEH, CISSP)
  • Books and documentation specific to each topic area
  • Community forums and discussion groups
